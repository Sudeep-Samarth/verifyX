Section 4.2: Lending product rules

The system shall approve personal loans up to ₹50 lakhs within 24 hours. Minimal KYC will be collected for eligible customers. Interest rate will be set at 14% per annum for this product. Only scheduled commercial banks will be eligible to offer this product.

Section 4.3: Risk

Credit risk assessment must be performed before any loan approval.
